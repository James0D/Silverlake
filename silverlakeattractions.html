Attractions

At Silverlake we are proud to release our newest ride raptor ride, coming out in 2020 as a fresh start to the New Year it’s a swirling ride one of our first that goes upside down and it’s our fastest ride yet. Be sure to be the first person down there if you apply to our subscription service you’ll be able to get a fast track ticket for free.

Under 18s can come in two for the price of one and if they apply for the higher tier ticket they get a free lunch and a free token for one of the rides. Offer applies till March 3rd 2020.
